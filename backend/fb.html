Rescue friends from the cold depths of space and try to stay alive!

Move the ship by moving the cursor.

Use the W, S, A, D keys to shoot.

Collect friends by touching them with your ship. Don't shoot them on accident!

If you collect/destroy a friend, they'll be notified of your kindness/tyranny.

Friends who have played the game appear as bosses. The higher the score, the tougher they are.
